Nutty Coco-Cacao Balls

Ingredients

  • 1 cup crushed Walnuts
  • 1 cup unsweetened Cacao Powder
  • 1 cup shredded Coconut
  • 4 Tbsp Honey
  • ½ cup Almond Butter
  • 4 Tbsp MCT oil or Coconut Oil

Directions

Mix all the ingredients together in a large container and make small balls out of it. They are ready to serve.
Feel free to roll them in shredded coconut for a nice presentation!
You have to refrigerate them and they are fresh and good for a week.



Keto Chocolate Cake

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Butter (softened) or MCT oil or Coconut oil
  • 1/4 cup granulated Erythritol sweetener
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tsp Vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups superfine Almond Flour
  • 1/3 cup Coconut Flour
  • 2 tsp Baking Powder
  • 1/4 tsp Xanthan Gum
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened premium Cacao Powder
  • 1 cup unsweetened Almond Milk

For Icing

  • Almond Butter Melted
  • Dark Chocolate

Directions

Preheat oven to 350 degrees (F) Grease a baking pan/ container with butter or non-stick spray.
Combine all of the ingredients in a blender and blend about 2 minutes, or until smooth or you can also stir it well if you don't have a food processor.
Spoon the batter into the greased pan (it will be thick) and smooth out with a spatula.
Bake at 350 degrees (F) for 40-60 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the middle comes out clean and the cake has risen slightly.
Remove and cool for 10 minutes before icing.
For Icing, mix the almond butter and melted dark chocolate with some honey (optional) and spread it evenly on the cake and it is ready to serve!

Almond Flour Crepes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Almond Flour
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 tsp Baking Powder
  • ¼ tsp Xantham Gum
  • ¼ cup Almond Milk
  • ½ chopped Onion Salt and Pepper to taste
  • 2-3 chopped Green Chillis
  • ¼ tsp Garlic Powder & ¼ tsp Ginger Powder
  • ¼ tsp Cayenne Pepper Avocado oil or any oil of your choice

Directions

Mix the almond flour, egg and almond milk Put the chopped onion, green chillies and other spices.
Stir it all well Heat the pan and put the oil Keep it to medium heat and spread the batter evenly on the pan.
Tip: Don't make the crepes on high heat. It has to be on medium heat to cook well.
You can serve the crepes with a side of veggies and any sauce of your choice. Enjoy!



Taco with a twist

Ingredients

  • ½ pound freshly chopped chicken
  • Any unrefined oil for stir-frying
  • ½ chopped onion
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • 1 sliced Avocado
  • 6-8 Lettuce Leaves
  • 2 Tbsp Mayonnaise
  • 2 Tbsp store bought Curry Masala

Directions

Mix the chicken, salt, oil and tandoori masala together and refrigerate it for at least 30 minutes.
Heat the pan and put some oil Stir fry the chicken on medium heat until cooked Lay all the lettuce leaves on a plate.
Put the cooked chicken on the lettuce.
Top it with sliced avocado and mayonnaise. Your low carb Taco is ready to eat!



Cauli Rice with Shrimps

Ingredients

  • 2 cup Cauliflower Rice
  • 1 chopped Onion
  • ½ chopped onion
  • 1 tsp Garlic Powder (fresh garlic can be used)
  • 1 tsp Ginger Powder (fresh ginger can be used)
  • 1 tsp Cayenne Pepper
  • Salt and Black Pepper to taste
  • 12- 15 pcs of tail free Shrimps
  • Olive oil or any unrefined cooking oil

Directions

Heat the pan and put some oil.
Put the raw shrimps with salt and pepper Cook it for 10 minutes or until cooked.
Put it aside Saute the chopped onion in oil until brown.
Now put the cauliflower rice, garlic, salt, black pepper, cayenne pepper, ginger and mix it well Cook it for 10 minutes.
After the rice is cooked, put the cooked shrimps and mix it well.
Top it with fresh alfa-alfa sprouts Your lunch or dinner is ready to serve!
